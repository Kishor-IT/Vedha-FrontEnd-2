Kayachikitsa (Internal Medicine)

Kayachikitsa focuses on diagnosing and treating internal diseases, ranging from metabolic disorders like diabetes to respiratory issues like asthma. It emphasizes restoring the body's balance through herbal remedies, diet, lifestyle modifications, and detoxification therapies.

Shalya Tantra (Surgery)

Shalya Tantra involves both surgical and non-surgical interventions for various ailments, including fractures, tumors, and organ disorders. Techniques like Kshara Sutra (medicated thread) for fistula and Panchakarma (detoxification) are employed alongside modern surgical procedures for optimal patient care.

Shalakya Tantra (ENT and Ophthalmology):

Shalakya Tantra specializes in treating disorders of the eyes, ears, nose, and throat. It includes treatments for conditions like cataracts, sinusitis, and hearing impairments using herbal medicines, nasal therapies, and specialized eye treatments like Tarpana (eye nourishment) and Nasya (nasal medication).

Kaumarabhritya (Pediatrics):

Kaumarabhritya focuses on pediatric healthcare, covering all aspects of child health from infancy to adolescence. It addresses common childhood illnesses, growth and development issues, and provides guidance on nutrition, immunization, and behavioral disorders to ensure holistic well-being from a young age.

Swasthavritta (Preventive and Social Medicine):

Swasthavritta emphasizes preventive healthcare and community medicine to promote public health and well-being. It encompasses lifestyle counseling, hygiene practices, environmental awareness, and disease prevention strategies to empower individuals and communities in maintaining optimal health and preventing the onset of illnesses.
